<objective>
Harden public invite registration and admin invite lifecycle backend behavior.

Purpose: treat invite codes as credential-like data while preserving LAN admin usability.
Output: throttled registration, generated-only high-entropy invite creation, audit redaction, lifecycle endpoints, and focused invite tests.
</objective>

<threat_model>
High severity threats block completion:
- T-01 invite brute force: repeated invalid registration attempts must reach `429`.
- T-02 weak invite credentials: generated invite codes must meet a clear minimum entropy policy and custom weak codes must not be accepted.
- T-03 secret persistence: audit logs must not store full invite codes.
- T-04 destructive history loss: used invite codes must not be hard-deleted.
- T-05 invalid reactivation: expired or exhausted invite codes must not become usable through reactivation alone.
</threat_model>
