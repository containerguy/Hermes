<objective>
Add profile, confirmed email-change, active-email uniqueness, and device-name backend behavior.

Purpose: make account management safe and usable without changing the broader frontend architecture.
Output: profile APIs, email-change flow, shared email uniqueness helper, device-name helper, and focused tests.
</objective>

<threat_model>
High severity threats block completion:
- T-01 account takeover: a requested new email must not receive login codes until verified.
- T-02 duplicate identity: one active email must not belong to multiple active accounts.
- T-03 stale sessions: admin email changes must revoke sessions according to Phase 1 decisions.
- T-04 privacy leakage: email-change and profile audit metadata must not include OTPs or unnecessary full email values.
- T-05 device confusion: users must not rename sessions owned by other users.
</threat_model>
