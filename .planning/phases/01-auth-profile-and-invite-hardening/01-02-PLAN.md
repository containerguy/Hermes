<objective>
Harden auth/session infrastructure and expose backend operations for abuse controls.

Purpose: make authentication safe enough for public invite registration without account enumeration or unbounded OTP attempts.
Output: persisted rate-limit helper/API, generic login behavior, and focused API tests.
</objective>

<threat_model>
High severity threats block completion:
- T-01 enumeration: `/api/auth/request-code` must not reveal whether a username exists through status or response shape.
- T-02 brute force: login-code, OTP verification, and invite registration must have practical persisted throttles by username/source.
- T-03 lockout recovery: admins must inspect/clear blocks and manage allowlisted LAN IPs.
</threat_model>
